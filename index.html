Leon Lufkin

I'm Leon, an undergrad at Yale majoring in Statistics & Data Science and Mathematics. I was inducted into Phi Beta Kappa in my junior year. I plan to graduate in Fall 2024.

I am broadly interested in inference and learning. To this end, I have largely been involved in research at the intersection of machine learning and neuroscience. I have had the fortune to work with John Lafferty (Yale) and Jonathan Cohen (Princeton) during my summers. In Fall 2023, I took time off from school to work as a Project Research Intern at the Gatsby Unit with Andrew Saxe (UCL).

I am also interested in more traditionally statistical problems. I worked with Yihong Wu (Yale) on studying shuffled linear regression, for which I won the Hartigan Prize from the Yale stats department. I recently presented our work at ISIT 2024.
