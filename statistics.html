Borel-Cantelli Lemma

If the sum of the probabilities of the events \(\{ E_n \}\) is finite, then the probability that infinitely many of them occur is zero.

\[ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \mathbb{P}\left( E_n \right) < \infty \implies \mathbb{P}\left( \limsup_{n\to\infty} E_n \right) \to 0. \]
